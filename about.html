Welcome to Magna Hire

Your Magnet for Top Talent

On average, employers spend over 6 weeks per hire, leading to delayed role fulfilment, disruption in work processes, and increased cost per hire.

Magna Hire was founded to tackle these hiring problems head-on. As a leading recruitment agency in India, we fuse intuitive AI, robust tech, and deep expertise to revolutionize how enterprises hire talent nationwide.

We provide end-to-end and full-stack recruitment solutions across verticals, spanning tech & non-tech hiring. Driven by results, our goal is to empower our global and national partners to easily access a pre-vetted talent pool, close open roles, and accelerate value realization from hiring investments.
